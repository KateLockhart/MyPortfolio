About Me:


I have a crazy personal notion that art and technology are one in the same. Both feel like creative and exciting adventures I undertake at the start of every project. First I found my love for art and design, before quickly becoming enamored with the endless potential and exponential growth of technology. Together they become a very powerful platform. Each project has it's own aesthetic and essence; creating that is something I thrive on.

I was born in San Francisco bay, but shortly moved to the great Midwest. Here I live and grow with many facets of my community. Drawing inspiration from the art, music, and many cultures around me. I've always focused on fostering human connection and find that drives me to make great collaborations and spectacular work.
